# GW-YOLO research status — 2026-07-21

## Executive status

The repository and remote experiment system are operational and reproducible, but the paper-level
scientific endpoint is not yet reached. The strongest completed result is a negative scaling result:
increasing unique analytic waveforms from 2,000 to 10,000 while holding the same 76 detector-noise
GPS blocks does not materially improve candidate ranking. This rules out an immediate 25k/50k
waveform-only expansion and promotes independent GPS/run/glitch diversity, multi-Q content and
aligned detector-set modelling instead.

No O4b/GWTC-5 strain has been opened for model, threshold, calibration or OOD selection. Current
catalog/image metrics are not reported as search recall. A paper claim remains gated on continuous
background FAR/IFAR, injection efficiency and `<VT>` at a frozen threshold.

## Completed evidence

- The leakage-safe legacy segmentation baseline, numeric data factory, manifest/hash provenance,
  atomic writes, validation-only checkpoint selection and CPU test suite are in place.
- Five 10k analytic-data seeds completed. The validation-selected seed has mean IoU 0.88810; the
  five-seed mean is 0.88087 with sample standard deviation 0.00678. This is a representation result,
  not a continuous-search result.
- A frozen 3,000-injection validation corpus and a group-safe 10,000-injection training corpus were
  materialized in verified O4a noise. The nested 2k/5k/10k candidate experiment retains every
  proposal and never counts repeated candidates as physical examples.
- The STFT candidate-pair scaling gate failed 3/4 predeclared checks. Fixed-update overall padded
  top-1 is 0.3322/0.3238/0.3322 at 2k/5k/10k and peak p90 remains about 4.34 s. The machine diagnosis
  is `waveform_scale_plateau_with_fixed_gps_support`; 25k/50k waveform-only scaling is blocked.
- A same-parent, same-900-update three-resolution STFT control was precommitted and completed. It
  changes overall top-1 from 0.3322 to 0.3339, SNR 8--15 top-1 from 0.6849 to 0.7123 and peak p90
  from 4.4769 s to 4.4028 s. Only the BNS/NSBH non-regression checks pass; all three improvement
  checks fail, so the arm is not allowed to scale to 5k.
- Gravity Spy single-IFO numeric materialization contains 1,505 train and 326 validation rows with
  split-safe physical IDs. A 93-task human weak-mask audit plan is frozen.
- Physical chirp+real-glitch mixtures contain 300 train and 100 validation rows and pass the joint
  waveform, injection, glitch, GPS and mixture cross-split audit.
- A one-seed single-IFO overlap fine-tune completed but failed the predeclared clean-retention gate:
  no epoch reached the required 0.95 clean chirp-IoU retention, and glitch-mask IoU remained zero.
  It is retained as a negative control and is not promoted to five seeds.
- A Blip-held-out OOD baseline used a threshold fitted only on 307 known validation examples. It
  held known abstention to 15/307 (4.89%) but abstained on 0/17 held-out Blips; unknown false
  acceptance is 17/17, with Wilson 95% interval 81.57%--100%, and diagnostic AUROC is 0.5294.
  Closed-set embedding confidence is therefore not an acceptable OOD policy.
- A second family was frozen by sample count before its scores were opened: Tomte contributes 27
  unknown rows over 10 GPS blocks, with 26 additional known rows in the same held-block evaluation.
  A precommitted supervised-contrastive/logit-energy arm improves diagnostic AUROC to 0.7735 and
  abstains on 5/27 Tomtes, but still falsely accepts 22/27 and falsely abstains on 3/26 known rows.
  It is directional evidence only and also fails promotion.
- The O4a endpoint now has an executable two-stage lock: corpus freezing records the immutable
  test manifest and predeclared access-log path without opening it; the one-time opening gate
  verifies the manifest hash, rejects physical-group overlap against every declared training,
  selection and calibration manifest, hashes the frozen model/config/threshold/OOD artifacts and
  atomically creates an exclusive access receipt. A second opening fails before test access.
- The independent-GPS scale test now has a causal paired arm and a frozen promotion gate. The same
  injection/waveform population is remapped onto GPS blocks disjoint from both the baseline and
  shared validation data, then compared under both fixed-update and fixed-epoch budgets with paired
  seeds and BBH/BNS/NSBH non-regression checks. This separates GPS/domain diversity from merely
  drawing another random waveform population of the same size.
- Morphology-only background shards can now be calibrated as a validation single-IFO trigger rate
  using true detector-time exposure. The report explicitly forbids a network FAR claim; it is an
  early rejection diagnostic while empirical 10 ms timing/coincidence remains unresolved.
- The publication PE adapter now rejects a nominal DINGO/AMPLFI comparison unless both backends use
  the identical injection set and byte-identical clean/contaminated/mask-conditioned inputs. It
  verifies input, contamination, mask-model and mask-policy hashes and enforces common prior,
  waveform, detector, calibration, hardware and end-to-end latency definitions across backends.
- A separate PE backend lock now pins DINGO v0.9.8 and AMPLFI v0.6.0 sources and refuses publication
  readiness until distinct CUDA environments, exact installed versions, domain-compatible model
  files and model metadata are all present and hash-frozen. The shared initial comparison is BBH on
  byte-identical H1/L1 4,096 Hz, 16-second source artifacts. The current committed model hashes are deliberately
  unresolved, so this is a truthful failing gate rather than a claim that package installation or
  external inference is complete.
- A standardized PE model-sidecar freezer now rejects test-selected checkpoints and binds each
  model to its validation selection report, training configuration/data manifest, common analysis
  prior, native conditioning, waveform conventions and inference-parameter set. The readiness audit
  re-hashes those artifacts and requires the DINGO/AMPLFI common prior, analysis waveform and output
  parameters to match before either result can enter a paper comparison.
- The official O4a DINGO release at `https://zenodo.org/records/21221337` is now the preferred
  model source rather than the older O3 archive. Its `SEOBNRv5PHM_v5PHM_H1L1` model is a 4.8 GB,
  high-mass H1/L1 precessing network. The downloaded settings checksum matches the published MD5
  `3dbd2867949d671fbfe8b65dde098654`; metadata records DINGO 0.5.8, epoch 225, ten million waveform
  samples, a 16-second/4,096 Hz window, 20--1,024 Hz analysis band and fourteen native parameters.
  Loading this model under the pinned 0.9.8 runtime is a compatibility test, not an assumption; a
  native 0.5.8 environment remains the fallback if serialized-model compatibility fails.
- AMPLFI v0.6's bundled CBC configuration instead uses H1/L1, 3 seconds at 2,048 Hz,
  `IMRPhenomPv2` and eight native output parameters. No reusable official checkpoint has yet been
  identified; its paper Zenodo record is queued for inspection after the core GWOSC downloads.
  Fairness therefore uses one 16-second/4,096 Hz source artifact, backend-native derived tensors,
  and an explicit common-parameter mapping rather than pretending native tensor shapes or output
  spaces are identical.

## Continuous-background gap

The fresh validation split has only 301 windows over 10 GPS blocks. Three hundred declared
positive-lag shifts yield 361,200 s = 0.01145 yr of observed background exposure. With zero events,
the 90% FAR upper limit would still be 201.17/yr. This is only 0.0497% of the exposure required for
a 0.1/yr claim. Even the optimistic all-unordered-pairs bound requires 13,479 valid dual-IFO
eight-second windows for a ten-year IFAR-scale claim. The current corpus is therefore an engineering
calibration shard, not a publishable FAR measurement.

The separate 5,000-injection O4a endpoint currently being materialized is a development-promotion
endpoint, not O4b/GWTC-5. It will remain unopened until the data-axis comparison, model checkpoint,
validation threshold and OOD policy are all frozen and the one-time opening receipt can be written.

## Schedule estimate

Assuming the remote GPU remains available and GWOSC downloads remain stable:

- aligned real-glitch acquisition and audits: 1--3 days;
- network-aware overlap baseline and OOD replacement: 3--7 days;
- background expansion plus executable FAR/IFAR/`<VT>` benchmark: 1--3 weeks;
- five-seed promoted model and deglitch ablations: 1--2 additional weeks;
- paired DINGO/AMPLFI inference and calibration study: 2--4 additional weeks;
- locked evaluation, robustness tables and manuscript-quality reruns: 2--4 additional weeks.

The earliest credible end-to-end evidence package is therefore about 6--8 weeks; a conservative
paper-ready estimate is 8--12 weeks. These are execution estimates, not a promise that a positive
effect will pass the frozen gates. A negative gate result triggers a bounded redesign and may extend
the schedule. Public O4b/GWTC-5 strain availability is an external dependency for the final locked
claim.
