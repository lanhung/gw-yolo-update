## Executive decision

The repository now has a nearly complete fail-closed implementation for validation evidence,
locked O4b/GWTC-5 search, OOD transfer, paired DINGO/AMPLFI parameter estimation and catalog
diagnostics. This is engineering readiness, not a scientific result. The real publication ledger is
still only 1/10 on validation evidence and 0/9 on locked-final evidence. O4b/GWTC-5 remains unopened.

The immediate scientific bottleneck is the source-file/GPS/glitch-disjoint aligned H1/L1/V1 corpus.
No training, threshold, calibration, OOD or PE decision is allowed to bypass that corpus audit. The
remote DAG is running and all downstream jobs wait for immutable upstream reports rather than
polling for partially written data.

## Remote execution DAG

The active chain is:

1. finish independent train/validation source shards;
2. merge the recovered and independent banks, resplit by connected source/GPS components and audit
   zero overlap;
3. generate source-safe clean+glitch overlaps;
4. run the frozen one-seed promotion, then five seeds only if it passes;
5. compare the promoted candidate model against the baseline on the independent O4a endpoint;
6. run fixed-epoch and fixed-update scaling controls;
7. stream the 800-pair base background and 80-pair extension;
8. execute raw/mask, calibration, OOD and paired-PE validation;
9. aggregate the 10-item validation ledger;
10. only then freeze and open the locked suite once.

At the snapshot the root acquisition process and all declared waiters are alive. GPU utilization is
zero because the only runnable stage is network/HDF5 acquisition. No unrelated process has been
terminated and `/root/GW-YOLO`, its weights and historical `runs` directory remain untouched.

## Definition of success

Success is not catalog hit rate or image mAP. The primary endpoint is paired change in recovered
`<VT>` at common frozen FAR on continuous background, with clean-injection non-inferiority and paired
bootstrap uncertainty. The distinct paper contribution is mask-preserving, detector-set-aware
glitch robustness and improved downstream posterior reliability under contamination. DINGO and
AMPLFI are compared only on shared PE coverage, bias, width, sky area, sample efficiency and latency,
never as detection-mAP baselines.

No claim is currently authorized. The locked corpus remains closed until all ten validation reports
replay by hash and pass their predeclared predicates.
